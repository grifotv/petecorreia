Thingful

Thingful is the world's first search engine for the Internet of Things, providing a unique geographical index of connected objects around the world, including energy, radiation, weather, and air quality devices as well as seismographs, iBeacons, ships, aircraft and even animal trackers. It also enables people and companies to claim and verify ownership of their things using a provenance mechanism, among other features.

I helped define the product, designed the identity and user interface, and also single-handedly developed the front-end of the application, which works across multiple devices.
